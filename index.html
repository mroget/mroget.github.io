My name is Mathieu Roget and I’m a PhD student in the team CaNa of the LIS (in Marseille, France) advised by Prof. Giuseppe Di Molfetta.

I’m currently studying quantum walk, distributed quantum computing and quantum machine learning. I also teach at Université Aix-Marseille.

Previously, I completed a bachelor and master degree in computer science from ENS Lyon and a bachelor degree in mathematics from Université Lyon 1.

My current research are focused on trying to tackle several problematics of distributed quantum computing (algorithms, implementation, quantum noise, …) using quantum walk and quantum cellular automata.
